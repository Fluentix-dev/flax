# Term-wise

A school, has 4 main roles:
- Student (learner),
- Faculty (teachers, professors, etc),
- School board (principle, administrator, etc who manages the school),
- Support Staff (external staff for example chiefs, janitors, etc that don't invole in educating the students directly, but they can also be staffs who fixes/repairs/manages school-related things for example IT-manager or librarian).
